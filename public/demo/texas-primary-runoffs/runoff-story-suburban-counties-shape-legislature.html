How Suburban Counties Could Shape the Next Texas Legislature

By Emily Foster · March 26, 2026 · 9 min read

Fast-growing suburban districts in Fort Bend, Travis, and Dallas counties feature competitive Texas House runoffs that could shift the tone of the next legislative session on issues from school finance to property taxes.

Candidates are courting swing voters who split their tickets in March, while party activists focus on turning out reliable primary voters a second time. The outcome may hinge on which campaigns best explain how Austin’s decisions affect local classrooms and county budgets.
