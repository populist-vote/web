Attorney General Runoff Goes the Distance on Crime and Border

By Laura Chen · March 27, 2026 · 7 min read

The runoff for Texas attorney general has become the most expensive statewide race on the ballot, with candidates and outside groups spending heavily on television ads focused on border security, violent crime, and consumer protection cases handled by the office.

Neither candidate cleared 50 percent in March, setting up a head-to-head contest that has drawn national interest from party donors and advocacy organizations on both sides.
