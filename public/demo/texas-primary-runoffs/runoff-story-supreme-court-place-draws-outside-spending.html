Supreme Court Place Race Draws Outside Spending From Both Sides

By David Park · March 26, 2026 · 6 min read

Two sitting appellate justices are competing in the runoff for an open seat on the Texas Supreme Court, and advocacy groups on opposite sides of tort reform and business regulation have poured millions into independent expenditures.

The race is one of several judicial runoffs on the ballot this May. Voters in Travis County and across the state will also decide contested races for intermediate appellate courts and local benches.
