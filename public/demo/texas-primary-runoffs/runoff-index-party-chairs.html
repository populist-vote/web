County & precinct party chair runoffs

Texas parties elect county chairs and precinct chairs to organize volunteers, run primaries, and turn out voters. When no candidate wins a majority in March, the runoff decides who leads the party apparatus in each jurisdiction—for Republicans, Democrats, or other parties holding a contested race. Our guides collect questionnaires on grassroots strategy, fundraising, and party priorities.
