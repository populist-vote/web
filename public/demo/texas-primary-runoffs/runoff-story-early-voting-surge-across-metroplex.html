Early Voting Surges Across the Metroplex Ahead of May 26

By Maria Santos · March 27, 2026 · 5 min read

Fort Bend County opened two new early-voting locations in Sugar Land and Missouri City after voters waited more than an hour on the first morning of the runoff period. In Travis County, election workers handed out bottled water to voters standing in line outside a South Austin library where a competitive Texas House runoff is topping the local ballot.

Harris County reported the single busiest first day of early voting for a runoff since at least 2014, with more than 28,000 ballots cast countywide. Dallas County saw similar energy in northern suburbs where a congressional runoff has attracted national attention.
